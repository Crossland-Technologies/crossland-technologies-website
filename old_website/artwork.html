3D Artwork

The famous addage 'a picture paints a thousand words' is true and especially so for 3D artwork.

We produce 3D imagery, animations and videos to help you clearly and concisely convey your idea.

We highly recommend you to use 3D artwork supports your conceptual design.

How it works (products / ideas / inventions):

  1. The first step is to model your product or idea in 3D, this means getting the physical dimensions correct.
  2. Once you approve the model dimensions we can work on appearance; get the colours and textures correct.
    • How far you go with the aesthetics of the model is personal preference, our guidance is to remember the purpose of the 3D artwork.
    • If the intention is to give a sense of scale or function only then aesthetics are not as important.
    • If the intention is for the model to be used in a product video then aesthetics are extremely important.
  3. Once the physical dimensions are correct, should you wish, you can also have your model 3D printed (full size or small scale).
  4. With the model dimensionally and aesthetically correct we can then use the model to produce the artwork you require:
    • Static images and/or diagrams (typically used to support conceptual design.)
    • Product placement images, where we place your 3D model into real photographs. (Typically used for investors & stakeholders, but also give a useful sense of scale.)
    • Animations, demonstrating how your idea works or is assembled. (Especially useful if the workings/assembly are not immediately apparent.)
    • Product Video, advertising your idea. (Typically used for websites and attracting customers).
  5. As well as individual discrete items we can also create scenes and background objects:
    • Static background images again can be used to give a good sense of scale.
    • Live construction plans - when updated regularly the 3D scene can be used to determine implementation routes and installation orders (e.g. process pipework and ducting).
    • Scene animations can convey construction order and/or fly by views.
    • Walkthrough videos can be created to show exactly how the final idea or concept will be implemented.

3D artwork not only gives a sense of legitimacy that does not come from 2D drawings but they can also be truly efficient means of conveying your idea to others.

Whether you want to wow your investors, teach new staff, support a design review or advertise; our 3D artwork services are here to help.
