Software Development

We have experience in developing software for a whole host of different platforms and technologies and hence we offer a completely hardware inspecific software development service.

Our software development service takes your software specification (or similar) and develops the application on whatever platform you choose. With all software development; we undertake rigorous internal testing as standard, to ensure the functionality meets your specification. We also support customer acceptance testing in a variety of formats to suit you and your organisation.

Hopefully from the examples above you can see our approach is to do what you need doing, instead of limiting you to what we can do. If your preferred technology isn't listed; we are always happy to pick up new skills - just get in touch.

If you don't know which platform you want, don't worry, we can advise you based on our experience. A lot of the decision comes down to preference and your organisation's history. Other factors include:

  • Turn around time - how fast do you need a prototype.
  • Complexity
  • Speed of response
  • Future proofing
  • Backwards compatibility

There's nothing quite like seeing the program function for the first time, that first glimmer of life for your idea.

Our rigorous approach to programming produces robust applications fast. Get in touch to see what we can do for you.
