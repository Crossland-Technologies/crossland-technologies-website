Hardware Production/Construction

Our hardware services are split into three areas:

Electrical Electro-Mechanical Mechanical
Purely electrical based work,
  • PCB Fabrication
  • Electrical Panel Assembly
  • Electrical Installation
  • Electrical Testing
 Combining mechanical and electrical components,
  • Field wiring and equipment connections
  • System Integration
  • Prototype Build
  • Functional Testing
 Purely mechanical based work,
  • Fabrication
  • System & sub-system assembly

Our in-house hardware capabilities are limited, but we do have excellent contacts that we rely on to outsource aspects which lie outside our capabilities.

Where your idea exceeds our in-house capabilities, we are happy to put you in touch with our contacts or outsource this on your behalf.

We strive to offer a turn-key service and under the majority of circumstances we will be able to develop your idea to prototype.

Our hardware services are flexible and can be co-ordinated with your needs. Examples of our hardware services include:

  • Embedded Systems / Small Scale (<1m2)
    • Where your idea can be package into a volume <1m2
    • We can produce the PCBs and/or electrical panel(s).
    • We can fabricate (in-house or outsource) the mechanical aspects.
    • We can assemble your electrical and mechanical components at our premises.
    • We can undertake functional testing at our premises.
    • We can ship your fully functioning prototype to you.

  • Package Systems / Medium Scale (1 - 10m2)
    • Where your idea is typically packaged on to a frame/skid that fits in an ISO container.
    • We can produce the PCBs and/or electrical panel(s).
    • We can accept your frame/skid at our premises.
    • We can install your electrical components at our premises.
    • We can install any remaining mechanical components at our premises.
    • We can undertake functional testing at our premises.
    • We can ship your fully functioning package to you.

  • Plant Control Systems / Large Scale(>10m2)
    • Where your idea is typically immovable.
    • We can produce the PCBs and/or electrical panel(s).
    • We can ship the electrical components to your premises.
    • We can install your electrical components at your premises.
    • We can undertake functional testing at your premises.

Get in touch to discuss which scenario works best for you.
