About Us

Crossland Technologies Ltd.

Introduction

Crossland Technologies was founded by two brothers in 2014 for the purpose of delivering automated systems to all. Automation is a key aspect of modern life and it looks set to continue to be weaved into the very fabric of society. Done properly; automation provides significant benefits, to owners, to employees and to consumers. However significant barriers; namely cost and understanding, prevent many from accessing these benefits.

Originally starting as design consultants we have extended our services to encompass the full automation life cycle.
We are genuinely passionate about automation and relish the differences that make each of our jobs unique.
Automation brings huge benefits; including the almost unlimited potential for technological advancement.

"Any sufficiently advanced technology is indistinguishable from magic." - Arthur C. Clarke 1973

We want to make magic; do you ?
