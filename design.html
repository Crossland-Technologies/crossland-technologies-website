Technical Design

Our technical design service provdes you with the expertise you need to get your system automated, from consultation to documentation. As with the conceptual design, the size of your design package will depend greatly upon the size, scale and complexity of your idea as well as the industry within which you operate.

Unlike the conceptual design which is concerned with explaining the idea, our technical design services are concerned with implementation - the hows and the whats:

  • How will the item be constructed.
  • How will the system and sub systems perform their function.
  • How will the item be operated.
  • How will the system respond to failure.
  • What materials are required.
  • What safety systems are required.
  • What is acceptable (pass) what is not acceptable (fail).

We have years of experience of delivering design packages for ideas and projects ranging from thousands of pounds to millions of pounds. We have experience working within highly regulated sectors and undertaken all aspects within the design life cycle.

A selection of typical design package deliverables are shown below, the list is by no means exhaustive:

If you are new to automation, we can advise you on the items you are likely to need.

If your organisation has different design deliverables and processes we are happy to accommodate these too.

Ultimately your design package needs enough information and detail to allow the idea / process to be built/constructed.
