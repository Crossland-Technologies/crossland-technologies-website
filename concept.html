Brain To Paper

The first step is to generate a conceptual design.

A conceptual design is a high level description of your idea and how it works; we want to get what's in your brain on to paper.

The scope and size of the conceptual design depends upon;

  • the size of your organisation,
  • the complexity of your idea.
  • the type of industry.

Conceptual designs are a crucial first step as they allow all stakeholders to operate from a common point.

For entrepreneurs and inventors conceptual deigns can provide credibility and legitimacy with investors.

For project managers conceptual designs provide re-assurance to stakeholders.

Our experience shows that getting the conceptual design right at the start is crucial for success.

Our conceptual design service consists:

  1. A kick-off meeting (face-to-face or video call) where we will discuss your idea.
  2. We will send you your tailored conceptual design questionnaire.
  3. Once the questionnaire is returned, we will produce your conceptual design.
  4. You then get chance to review your new conceptual design.
  5. We will update your design with any comments you may have.
  6. With your conceptual design you're ready to approach the world to make your idea a reality.
